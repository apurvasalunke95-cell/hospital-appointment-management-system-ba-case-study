# 🏥 Hospital Appointment Management System

## Business Analyst Portfolio Project

**Author:** Apurva Tukaram Salunke

---

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates the end-to-end work of a Business Analyst in improving a hospital's appointment booking process. The goal is to replace a manual scheduling system with a digital appointment management solution that improves operational efficiency and patient experience.

---

## Business Problem

The hospital currently uses phone calls and manual registers to schedule appointments. This leads to:

- Long patient waiting times
- Double bookings
- Missed appointments
- Poor visibility of doctor schedules
- Increased workload for reception staff
- Limited reporting and analytics

---

## Proposed Solution

Develop a web-based Hospital Appointment Management System that enables patients to book appointments online while allowing doctors and hospital staff to efficiently manage schedules.

---

## Business Goals

- Reduce appointment booking time
- Eliminate double bookings
- Improve patient satisfaction
- Reduce administrative workload
- Increase doctor utilization
- Provide real-time reporting

# 6. Project Scope

## In Scope

The Hospital Appointment Management System will include:

- Patient Registration
- Patient Login
- Doctor Registration
- Doctor Login
- Receptionist Login
- Online Appointment Booking
- Appointment Cancellation
- Appointment Rescheduling
- Doctor Availability Management
- Appointment History
- Email/SMS Appointment Reminders
- Admin Dashboard
- Reports and Analytics

## Out of Scope

The following features are not included in this release:

- Online Payment
- Video Consultation
- Pharmacy Management
- Laboratory Management
- Electronic Medical Records (EMR)
- Insurance Claim Processing

# 7. Stakeholder Analysis

| Stakeholder | Responsibilities | Interest |
|--------------|------------------|----------|
| Patient | Book, cancel and reschedule appointments | High |
| Doctor | Manage schedule and appointments | High |
| Receptionist | Schedule appointments and assist patients | High |
| Hospital Administrator | Manage users and monitor reports | High |
| IT Team | Develop and maintain the system | Medium |
| Hospital Management | Monitor KPIs and project success | High |

---

# 8. Assumptions

- Patients have internet access.
- Doctors will update their availability regularly.
- Hospital staff will receive training before system launch.
- The system will be available 24/7 except during maintenance.
- Users will have valid login credentials.

---

# 9. Constraints

- Project timeline: 6 months
- Budget is limited.
- Must comply with healthcare data privacy regulations.
- The application must support modern web browsers.
- Initial release will support English only.

---

# 10. Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| Resistance to new system | High | Conduct user training |
| Incorrect requirements | High | Regular stakeholder reviews |
| Delays in development | Medium | Weekly sprint planning |
| Data migration issues | Medium | Perform testing before deployment |
| System downtime | High | Regular backups and monitoring |

---

# 11. Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if:

- Appointment booking time is reduced by at least 50%.
- Double bookings are eliminated.
- Patient satisfaction increases.
- Doctors can manage schedules without conflicts.
- Appointment reminder delivery rate exceeds 95%.
- Administrative workload is significantly reduced.
