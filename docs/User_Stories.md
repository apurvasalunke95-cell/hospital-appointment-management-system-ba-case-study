# User Stories

## Project: Hospital Appointment Management System

Prepared By: Apurva Tukaram Salunke

---

# Epic 1: User Registration & Login

### US-001: Patient Registration

**As a** new patient  
**I want** to create an account  
**So that** I can book appointments online.

**Priority:** High

---

### US-002: Patient Login

**As a** registered patient  
**I want** to log in securely  
**So that** I can access my appointments.

**Priority:** High

---

# Epic 2: Appointment Management

### US-003: Search Doctor

**As a** patient  
**I want** to search for doctors by specialty and availability  
**So that** I can choose the right doctor.

**Priority:** High

---

### US-004: Book Appointment

**As a** patient  
**I want** to book an available appointment slot  
**So that** I can receive medical consultation.

**Priority:** High

---

### US-005: Cancel Appointment

**As a** patient  
**I want** to cancel my appointment  
**So that** another patient can use the available slot.

**Priority:** Medium

---

### US-006: Reschedule Appointment

**As a** patient  
**I want** to change my appointment date and time  
**So that** I can visit the hospital at a convenient time.

**Priority:** Medium

---

# Epic 3: Doctor Module

### US-007: View Appointment Schedule

**As a** doctor  
**I want** to view my appointment schedule  
**So that** I can prepare for upcoming consultations.

**Priority:** High

---

### US-008: Update Availability

**As a** doctor  
**I want** to update my available time slots  
**So that** patients can book only available appointments.

**Priority:** High

---

# Epic 4: Receptionist Module

### US-009: Manage Appointments

**As a** receptionist  
**I want** to create, update, and cancel appointments  
**So that** I can assist patients efficiently.

**Priority:** High

---

# Epic 5: Administrator Module

### US-010: View Reports

**As an** administrator  
**I want** to view appointment and patient reports  
**So that** I can monitor hospital performance.

**Priority:** Medium
