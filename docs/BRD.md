# 1. Introduction

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to develop a Hospital Appointment Management System that enables patients to book appointments online while allowing doctors and hospital staff to efficiently manage appointment schedules. The system aims to reduce manual effort, improve scheduling accuracy, and enhance the overall patient experience.

---

# 2. Business Background

The hospital currently schedules appointments using phone calls and paper registers. This manual process often leads to long waiting times, scheduling conflicts, missed appointments, and increased administrative workload.

The hospital management has decided to implement a digital appointment management system to streamline operations and improve service quality.

---

# 3. Business Problem Statement

The existing appointment booking process has several challenges:

- Manual appointment scheduling
- Long patient waiting times
- Double bookings
- Difficulty checking doctor availability
- High administrative workload
- Lack of appointment reminders
- Limited reporting for hospital management

---

# 4. Business Objectives

The objectives of the project are:

- Reduce appointment booking time by 50%
- Eliminate double bookings
- Improve patient satisfaction
- Reduce administrative workload
- Increase doctor utilization
- Improve reporting and decision-making
- Digitize the appointment booking process

---

# 5. Business Goals

The Hospital Appointment Management System should:

- Allow patients to book appointments online
- Allow doctors to manage their schedules
- Allow receptionists to schedule appointments
- Send appointment reminders
- Maintain appointment history
- Generate reports for hospital management
