# Risk Register

## Project: Hospital Appointment Management System

Prepared By: Apurva Tukaram Salunke

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Risk Register identifies potential risks that may impact the successful delivery of the Hospital Appointment Management System. It also defines mitigation strategies to reduce their impact.

---

| Risk ID | Risk Description | Probability | Impact | Priority | Mitigation Strategy | Owner | Status |
|----------|------------------|-------------|--------|----------|---------------------|-------|--------|
| R-001 | Incomplete business requirements | Medium | High | High | Conduct regular stakeholder meetings and requirement reviews | Business Analyst | Open |
| R-002 | Delay in development | Medium | High | High | Weekly sprint planning and progress tracking | Project Manager | Open |
| R-003 | Resistance to new system by staff | Medium | Medium | Medium | Conduct user training and awareness sessions | Project Manager | Open |
| R-004 | Data migration errors | Low | High | Medium | Perform data validation and backup before migration | Technical Lead | Open |
| R-005 | System performance issues | Low | High | Medium | Conduct performance testing before release | QA Team | Open |
| R-006 | Security vulnerabilities | Low | High | High | Implement secure authentication and penetration testing | Development Team | Open |
| R-007 | Internet connectivity issues | Medium | Medium | Medium | Provide offline contingency procedures | IT Team | Open |
| R-008 | Scope creep | Medium | High | High | Follow formal change request process | Business Analyst | Open |

# Risk Review Process

- Review risks during every sprint review.
- Update the risk status after mitigation activities.
- Escalate high-priority risks to the Project Manager and Sponsor.
- Close risks once mitigation actions are completed successfully.
